Source Methodology

Methodology

The calculator divides the total cost of a government project by the average UK household's lifetime tax contribution:

Project Cost ÷ £1,277,580 = Households

The figure of £1,277,580 is the average UK household's total tax contribution over a lifetime, sourced from the TaxPayers' Alliance.

The result is rounded to the nearest whole number. It represents how many households would need to dedicate their entire lifetime of tax payments to fund a single project.

Project costs are sourced individually — click "Source" on each project card for details.
